About us

BarberBary is a digital brand studio for barbershops and nail studios in Israel. We think every great shop deserves more than a slot inside somebody else's booking app — it deserves its own website, its own app, and its own AI answering clients on WhatsApp.

Every build is designed one-on-one with the shop owner, on a foundation we've engineered for one industry only. That's why no two BarberBary shops look alike — and why all of them run flawlessly.

Based in Rishon LeZion. Working across Tel Aviv and the center. Taking a limited number of new shops each year.
